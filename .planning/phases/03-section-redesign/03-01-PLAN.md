# Phase 3.1: Hero & Stats Redesign — Plan

## Objective

Redesign the Hero and Stats sections with a minimalist brutalist aesthetic. These are the first sections visitors see—they must create immediate impact through bold typography and data-forward presentation.

## Prerequisites

- Phase 2 complete (design tokens established)
- Design utilities available: `text-shadow-glow`, `shadow-glow`, typography classes
- Understanding of brutalist principles from Phase 2

## Design Direction

### Brutalist Hero Principles
The hero should feel **confident and stripped-down**:
- Typography dominates—no decorative elements
- Stats integrated directly (remove duplication)
- Single, unmissable CTA
- Remove visual noise (grid overlays, spotlight gradients)
- Whitespace breathes

### Data-Forward Stats
Stats should feel like a **dashboard readout**:
- Monospace for numbers (JetBrains Mono)
- Minimal labels, maximum data
- Consider: one-line horizontal format vs stacked
- Remove testimonial from Stats (move to separate social proof section later)
